FAQ

Answers Before You Visit

What eye conditions does Dr. Ahuja treat at Markanday Eye Care?

Dr. Markanday Ahuja treats cataract, glaucoma, retina concerns, cornea conditions, refractive errors, pediatric eye problems, squint, eyelid concerns, and routine vision issues at the Rohtak clinic.

Is cataract surgery painful? What should I expect?

Cataract surgery is usually performed with numbing eye drops or local anesthesia, so most patients feel pressure rather than pain. Dr. Ahuja explains the steps, lens options, precautions, and follow-up plan before surgery.

Am I a good candidate for LASIK surgery?

LASIK suitability depends on your age, spectacle power stability, corneal thickness, dry eye status, and retinal health. A detailed LASIK evaluation helps decide whether LASIK, SMILE, PRK, or glasses are safer for you.

How long does cataract surgery take?

The surgical procedure is often completed within a short time, but patients should keep extra time for preparation and recovery observation. Your exact plan is discussed after the cataract evaluation.

What is the recovery time after LASIK?

Many patients notice clearer vision within one to two days, though dryness and mild fluctuation can continue for a short period. Follow-up visits and prescribed drops are important for stable healing.

Does the clinic accept health insurance or Ayushman Bharat?

Insurance and scheme coverage can change by policy and treatment type. Please call +91 98965 53294 or +91 89509 81653 before visiting so the clinic can confirm current documentation and eligibility.

What is the approximate cost of cataract surgery in Rohtak?

Cost depends on the lens selected, the eye condition, and any additional risk factors. After examination, Dr. Ahuja's team shares a clear estimate so your family can make an informed decision.

How do I book an appointment at Markanday Eye Care?

You can call +91 98965 53294, call +91 89509 81653, message on WhatsApp, or use the appointment form on this website. The clinic confirms your final time by phone.

What are the clinic's working hours and location?

Markanday Eye Care is located at 2481/2478, Sector-1 Main Road, HUDA Sector-1, Rohtak, Haryana 124001. The clinic is open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM and closed on Sunday.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Bring your old glasses, previous prescriptions, current medicines, diabetes or blood pressure records, past surgery notes, and any insurance documents. Children should bring prior school vision reports if available.

Is it safe to get eye surgery at a clinic in Rohtak instead of Delhi?

Safety depends on the surgeon, diagnosis, equipment, sterilization, and follow-up care, not only the city. Dr. Ahuja's experience allows many patients from Rohtak, Jhajjar, and Bahadurgarh to receive expert care locally.

What is glaucoma and how is it treated?

Glaucoma is often linked to eye pressure and can damage the optic nerve silently. Treatment may include eye drops, monitoring, laser, or surgery depending on severity and progression.

At what age should children first get their eyes tested?

Children should be checked early if parents notice squinting, watering, screen difficulty, headaches, white reflex, or poor school performance. Even without symptoms, a routine eye test before school age is useful.

Can diabetic patients safely undergo eye surgery?

Many diabetic patients can safely undergo eye surgery after proper retinal evaluation and sugar control. Dr. Ahuja checks for diabetic retinopathy and coordinates timing to reduce avoidable risk.

What advanced technology does Markanday Eye Care use for diagnosis and treatment?

The clinic uses modern diagnostic and treatment support for cataract, retina, glaucoma, cornea, and refractive care, including imaging, pressure assessment, corneal mapping, and advanced cataract planning tools.
